## 🏗️ **Apache Software Foundation (ASF)**

### 🆕 Apache Livy Graduates to Top-Level Project! (June 4)
**Apache® Livy** — a REST service for Apache Spark™ — has officially become an ASF Top-Level Project (TLP)! Livy makes it easy to interact with Spark clusters via REST API, enabling interactive Spark sessions, batch submissions, and seamless integration with BI tools. Big win for the Spark ecosystem! 🔥

### 📰 ASF Plus One Newsletter: May 2026 (May 28)
The latest monthly roundup covers:

## 🌿 **Linux Foundation (LF)**

### 🛡️ OpenSSF: CRA Compliance — Wake-up Call (June 3)
**"Taking Stock of the State of European Cyber Resilience Act (CRA) Compliance"** — a sobering analysis of where the open source ecosystem stands on CRA readiness. The OpenSSF is ringing the alarm: organizations need to act **now** to prepare for CRA requirements. Cyber Resilience Act compliance is a major theme across LF right now.

### 🤖 TODO Group Launches Agentic AI for OSPOs Working Group (May 19)
The **TODO Group** (under LF) has launched a new **Agentic AI to Empower OSPOs Working Group** — exploring AI agent workflows and how Open Source Program Offices should manage and leverage them. This is a sign of how fast agentic AI is becoming an OSPO concern.

### 💼 Navigating the 2026 Tech Talent Landscape (May 26)
LF Research published findings on the **AI Skills Crisis** — the takeaway: upskilling is our best answer. AI isn't replacing technical workers wholesale, but the skills landscape is shifting fast.

### 🏗️ Project Glasswing (April 7)

## 📡 **OpenInfra Foundation**

### ☁️ OpenStack: Gazpacho → Hibiscus Cycle in Full Swing
The **OpenStack 2026.1 "Gazpacho"** release dropped April 1, and the community is now deep into the **2026.2 "Hibiscus"** cycle. The April 2026 PTG brought together **35+ teams** and hundreds of contributors to plan the next release. Key areas of focus: simplifying operations, VM migration, workload mobility.

### ⭐ StarlingX 12.0 (May 5)
Right on time for **mixed-hardware edge deployments**! Highlights:
- Added **OIDC support** for modern authentication
- Sharpens **edge cloud management** capabilities
- Covered by Network World and SDxCentral

### 🏢 Yahoo! Japan / LY Corporation: Hyperscale OpenStack
**Consolidating 164 OpenStack clusters into one** — massive infrastructure rationalization project covered by The Register (April 7). A testament to OpenStack's scalability at hyperscale.

### 🚚 VMware Migration Guide Published
The OpenInfra community released a comprehensive **VMware-to-OpenStack Migration Guide** building on last year's whitepaper. Includes case studies, reference architectures, and practical migration strategies. This is timely as VMware licensing changes continue to drive interest.

### 🗓️ Next PTG: October 12–16
The next Project Teams Gathering will take place after Hibiscus release — registration details coming soon.

### **Trending in OpenInfra:** 🏗️ **LF consolidation** (board intent to join LF), OpenStack for AI infrastructure, Edge Computing (StarlingX), Secure Containers (Kata Containers), VMware migration wave
