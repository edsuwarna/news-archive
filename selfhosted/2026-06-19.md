## 🔥 Top Picks

### 1. 🧠 Ongrid — AI Ops Agent (⭐ 281)
**An ops AI agent that understands your infrastructure, finds root cause, and fixes it — right from Slack, Telegram, Lark, or DingTalk.**

- **GitHub:** https://github.com/ongridio/ongrid
- **Tags:** `AI-Ops` `Incident-Response` `ChatOps` `SRE` `Observability`
- **Created:** May 23, 2026 — super fresh!
