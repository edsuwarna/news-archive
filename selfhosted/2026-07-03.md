```
Tags: AIOps, ChatOps, Root-Cause-Analysis, Incident-Response, RAG
GitHub: https://github.com/ongridio/ongrid
License: Apache-2.0 | Lang: Go
```

**Why it matters:** Traditional alerting tells you CPU is high. Ongrid tells you _why_ CPU is high, _which_ deploy caused it, and _rolls it back_ — all from a Slack message. PagerDuty with a brain.
