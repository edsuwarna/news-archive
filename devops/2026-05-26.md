### 1. 🏆 OpenTelemetry Achieves CNCF Graduated Project Status
**OpenTelemetry officially graduated from the CNCF, cementing its position as the de facto standard for collecting telemetry data.** The project, donated to the CNCF in 2019 from the merger of OpenTracing and OpenCensus, now handles traces, metrics, logs, and profiles. CNCF CTO Chris Aniszczyk noted OTel will play a major role in instrumenting AI agents, and that the ecosystem is increasingly using eBPF as another telemetry source exposed in OTel format. A Futurum Group survey found 36% of organizations plan to spend over $1M on observability in 2026.
🔗 https://devops.com/opentelemetry-achieves-cncf-graduated-project-status/
