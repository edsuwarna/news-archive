**1️⃣ AWS Replaces Fat-Tree Data Center Networks with Random Graph Theory, Cutting Routers by 69%**
> In one of the most impactful data center networking changes in years, AWS announced it has replaced traditional fat-tree topologies with a random graph-based network architecture across its data centers. The shift eliminates the need for core spine switches and reduces router count by 69%, dramatically lowering cost and power consumption while maintaining performance. This is a landmark infrastructure engineering achievement that rethinks decades-old networking conventions.
🔗 https://www.infoq.com/news/2026/06/aws-random-graph-data-center/
